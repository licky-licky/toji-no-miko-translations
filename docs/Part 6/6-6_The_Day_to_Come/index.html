Kanayamahime: Please leave it to me.

Mihono: ...Alright, Kanayamahime. What should I do?
Kanayamahime: Please touch Kagutsuchi.
Mihono: I just have to touch her...? Okay, here goes, Kagutsuchi!!
Kagutsuchi: !!
